Emergency Help

Call 999 in emergencies for Police, Fire, ambulance and more

112 is the international emergency number this may also be useful

NHS 111 can help if you have an urgent medical problem and you’re not sure what to do.

https://www.nhs24.scot/

For Mental Health emergencies these may be useful although if there is a threat to life or serious injury call 999 immediately.
